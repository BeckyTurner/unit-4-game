Crystals Collector!!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. This will be the score to match.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on the crystal, you will add a specific amount to your score.

You win the game by matching your score to the random number, and you lose the game if your score goes over the random number.

You need to figure out the value of each crystal by clicking on it.

Each time the game is reset, the values of the crystals will change.
